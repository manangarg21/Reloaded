Want to know about our site?

Why did we create this site?

This site was created by the sole purpose of providing an online platform for rating songs by the general public. We all have seen a movies imDb platform which is considered to be a significant criteria when it comes to judging a movie. Well, we thought why don't we create a similar portal for songs...
Imagine you could have a site, where you can rate the songs based on your likings, give critiques about the artists, their albums and rank them based on their popularity in the recent days. It would be such a havoc to create a separate site for this purpose and simultaneously listening to the songs. So we present to you: ReLOADED
Also on a totally unrelated note, we had to finish our phases of ISS project.

What is this site? What are it's features?

On this site, you can rate the artists based on their albums, individual songs, and vibing to these songs with your friends at the same time. There is an "Artists Page" where you can see which music diva is in the hits currently and all of their albums compiled at a place. By clicking on these artists, you'll be redirected to their personalized page, where you can view all of their albums and get all sort of details about the same.
On our "Home Page" you can find the top ranking albums, songs and artists this month. There is a "nav bar" with which you can visit any part of our website. You can also find a redirecting footer to this page when you're on other sites.
We have recently added a "Search Page" where you can search for your favourite albums, songs, and artists, and can hear a preview of the search results. You can also filter out the duration of the songs and listen to explicit songs too ._.
